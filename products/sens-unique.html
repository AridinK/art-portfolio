About This Work

Sens Unique presents a subway staircase flooded with sunlight towards which the viewer emerges from the dark. The brush strokes enhance the visual haze in which the figures travel to their respective direction. Through its use of colour, light, and direction, the painting reflects the rhythm of public life, drawing on themes of anonymity, movement and transitions.
