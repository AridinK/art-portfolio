About This Work

Été au Parc de la Gorge captures a quiet summer moment shaped by light and movement. The flowing water, reflections, and surrounding greenery create a sense of calm continuity, where subtle shifts in color and texture suggest both stillness and motion. The scene invites the viewer to slow down and observe the natural rhythm of the landscape.
